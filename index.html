Shu Liu | 刘澍

Biography

Shu Liu received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. in 2018 and 2022 from the Eidgenössische Technische Hochschule Zürich (ETHZ), under supervision of Prof. Helmut Bölcskei and Prof. Elgar Fleisch, respectively. He received his B.Sc. in 2015 in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Technical University of Munich (TUM) under the supervision of Prof. Thomas Eibert.

Research Interests

My research focuses on ubiquitous and mobile computing. The vision is to integrate sensing, computing and decision-making into everyday IoT infrastructures for intelligent individual and urban services. My current research topics include:

  • Internet of Things

  • Digital Health

  • Intelligent Vehicles
