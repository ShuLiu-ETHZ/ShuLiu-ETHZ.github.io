Shu Liu received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. in 2018 and 2022 from the Eidgenössische Technische Hochschule Zürich (ETHZ or ETH Zurich), under supervision of Prof. Helmut Bölcskei and Prof. Elgar Fleisch, respectively. He received his B.Sc. in 2015 from Technical University of Munich (TUM) under the supervision of Prof. Thomas Eibert.

/ / /


Research Interests

My research focuses on robust machine learning and ubiquitous computing in IoT. The vision is to integrate sensing, explainability and knowledge discovery into everyday IoT infrastructures for smart cities and Industry 4.0. My current research topics include:

  • Ubiquitous Computing and Machine Learning

  • Digital Health

  • Intelligent Transporation Systems
